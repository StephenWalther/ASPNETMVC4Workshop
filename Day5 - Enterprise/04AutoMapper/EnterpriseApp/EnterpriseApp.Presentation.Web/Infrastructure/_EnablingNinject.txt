

1. Install-Package Ninject.MVC3 in your MVC4 app
2. Install-Package Ninject in your Service app

3. Add NinjectDependencyResolver class
4. Register NinjectDependencyResolver class in NinjectWebCommon.CreateKernel() method

5. Create the DIBindings (Ninject module) in your Presentation layer
6. Create the DIBindings (Ninject module) in your Service layer
7. Load both DIBindings in the NinjectWebCommon.RegisterServices() method
